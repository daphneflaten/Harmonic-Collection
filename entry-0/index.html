“Dogs of NY” is a theme that explores the beauty of people and their dogs. More specifically, going for walks with your dog. A simple chore for most people, but when you really examine the dynamics of a person with their dog, the relationship can be insightful and reveal much about the person. Sometimes it's physical, like a pampered dog with a clean-cut owner; but other times the pair can be mentally similar, such as an obedient dog with a patient owner. There's several things you can look at to see how a dog can match their owner, like is it an expensive dog, or was it rescued? How would the dog's fur match their expensive couch in the living room? Is this a dog that would go hiking with their owner, or binge an entire series with them on netflix? The beauty of dog walking is that you don't really know how these people live, but when you imagine the dynamics between them and their dog you get a glimpse into how they might live their life.
